Nathan John Feuerstein, better known by his stage name NF, is a highly acclaimed American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Born on March 30, 1991, in Gladwin, Michigan, NF has become renowned for his raw and honest lyrics that touch upon deeply personal themes such as mental health, relationships, and self-worth. His music is often characterized by its emotional depth and powerful storytelling, which resonate with listeners worldwide.

NF's rise to fame began with the release of his debut album, Mansion in 2015. Since then, he has continued to captivate audiences with his subsequent albums, each showcasing his growth as an artist. NF's music has earned him numerous awards and accolades, solidifying his place in the rap and hip-hop industry.

GMA Dove Award
are presented by the Gospel Music Association (GMA) to recognize outstanding achievement in the Christian music industry. Established in 1969, these awards celebrate artists and music across various Christian genres, including rock, pop, hip hop, country, and urban contemporary gospel1. The awards are often referred to as the "Grammy Awards of Christian music" and are held annually in Nashville, Tennessee
Billboard Music Awards
The Billboard Music Awards are presented by Billboard magazine and honor artists based on their performance on the Billboard charts, which include album sales, digital downloads, streaming, radio airplay, and social engagement. These awards have been held annually since 1990 and cover a wide range of music genres, from pop and rock to rap and country2. The Billboard Music Awards are known for their star-studded performances and are typically held in May.
